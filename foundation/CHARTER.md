# Class Q Foundation Charter

The Class Q Foundation exists to steward the Class Q Open Standard — a unified, generation‑proof architecture for 1‑bit audio, transport, DSP, and GaN power conversion.

---

## Mission

- Maintain and evolve the Class Q specifications  
- Ensure interoperability across vendors and industries  
- Promote open, royalty‑free adoption  
- Provide reference implementations  
- Support academic and industrial research  
- Protect the integrity and openness of the standard  

---

## Scope

The Foundation oversees:
- Q‑DSD (file format)  
- Q‑Link (transport)  
- Q‑Core (DSP engine)  
- Q‑DSP (plugin API)  
- Q‑Time (timecode)  
- Q‑Stream (broadcast)  
- Q‑Disc (archival)  
- Q‑Amp (power stage)  
- Q‑IEM (monitoring)  
- Q‑Venue (live sound)  
- Q‑Studio (studio)  
- Q‑DRM (optional DRM)
