# Class Q Foundation Membership

Membership is open to individuals, companies, researchers, and institutions who support the mission of the Class Q Open Standard.

---

## Membership Types

### Individual Contributor
- Open to anyone  
- Participate in Working Groups  
- Submit proposals and PRs  

### Academic Member
- Universities and research labs  
- Access to reference designs  
- Collaboration on papers and studies  

### Industry Member
- Manufacturers, studios, venues, broadcasters  
- Participate in interoperability testing  
- Early access to draft specifications  

### Steering Member
- Elected representatives  
- Voting rights on major decisions  
- Oversight of Working Groups  

---

## Expectations

Members agree to:
- uphold the open nature of the standard  
- avoid proprietary contamination  
- contribute constructively  
- respect the Code of Conduct  

---

## Fees

Membership is free during the initial development phase.  
Future fees (if any) will be used solely to support Foundation operations.
